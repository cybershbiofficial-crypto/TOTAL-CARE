**1. Core Statement**
**TOTAL CARE must operate within the strict labor and construction compliance mandates of the UAE.**

**2. Details**

- **Regulatory context**: UAE labor laws (MOHRE), Dubai Municipality construction codes.
- **Compliance mandate**: Certified engineering standards are a core marketing and operational requirement. Privacy policy must adhere to local data protection laws (UAE DPL).
- **Institutional / stakeholder relations**:
  - DO: Emphasize "Certified Engineering".
  - DO NOT: Promise un-permitted structural changes.

**3. Why it matters**

- Misrepresenting certifications or labor compliance on the platform can result in severe legal and operational penalties in Dubai.
