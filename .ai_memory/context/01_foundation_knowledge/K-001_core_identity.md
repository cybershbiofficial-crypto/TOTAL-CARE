**1. Core Statement**
**TOTAL CARE is a luxury-oriented provider of Premier Labour Supply and High-End Renovation Services based in Dubai, focused on unmatched craftsmanship and certified engineering.**

**2. Details**

- **Brand definition**: TOTAL CARE (Organization: TOTAL CARE Dubai), "Elevating commercial and residential spaces across Dubai".
- **Core intent**: Serve enterprise clients, commercial property managers, and luxury residential owners by providing top-tier labour and renovation services.
- **Core innovation**: Combining certified engineering precision with luxury craftsmanship and dedicated VIP service.
- **Retention / moat mechanism**: High-end luxury appeal, impeccable quality, direct WhatsApp-based VIP support, and strong client relationships.
- **Positioning — DO**: Use premium, high-end language. Focus on "luxury", "certified engineering", and "craftsmanship".
- **Positioning — DO NOT**: Frame as cheap, basic, or budget-friendly labour supply. Never rewrite Lovable git history.

**3. Why it matters**

- Ensuring all AI agents and developers strictly adhere to the luxury branding and premium positioning of the project.
