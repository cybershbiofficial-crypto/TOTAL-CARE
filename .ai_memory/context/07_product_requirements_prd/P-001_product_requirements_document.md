**1. Core Statement**
**The PRD for TOTAL CARE outlines the necessary components for a B2B/B2C premium labour supply and renovation services platform.**

**2. Details**

- **Key Epics**:
  1. _Public Landing Experience_: Hero section, Process, Services, Testimonials, Footer.
  2. _CMS Admin Panel_: Secure Supabase-backed management for dynamic content.
  3. _Instant Lead Generation_: Global WhatsApp integration for instantaneous contact.
- **User Roles**:
  - _Guest (Client)_: Browses services, views gallery, initiates WhatsApp chat.
  - _Admin (Staff)_: Manages content via `/admin`.
- **System Scope**: The MVP encompasses the complete marketing site and the admin CMS.
- **Boundaries**: E-commerce, direct payment gateways, and user accounts for clients are strictly out of scope.

**3. Why it matters**

- Defines the "done" state of the project and prevents scope creep.
